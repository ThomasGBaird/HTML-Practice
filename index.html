My cats

My wife, Hannah, and I have three cats. Their names are Biscuit, Beignet, and Fritter. Here is a little bit about each of them.

Biscuit

Biscuit is the oldest of our three cats. We got him in the Fall of 2015 from a lady that Hannah knows who found him at her house. He has a grey colored coat with short, wispy hair.

Some of Biscuit's favorite things to do are...

  • Run around in the backyard
  • Play with his little brother, Fritter
  • Nap with Hannah
  • Hide under the bed when it's time to go to bed

Beignet

Beignet is the second oldest of our cats, and the only girl. We adopted her and Fritter at the same time from Reidville Road Animal Hospital. She is about a month older than Fritter, but probably about a year younger than Biscuit. She has a blond colored coat with short, soft hair.

Here are some of Beignet's favorite activities

  • Napping (and lots of it)
  • Eating (she's the fattest of our cats)
  • Licking Hannah's face and fingers
  • Sitting in her kittie chair by the window

Fritter

Fritter is the youngest cat in the bunch, be he is by far the roundest. He has a little head but a big heart. Some days he gets needy and wants lots of attention, but others he is happy to just do his own thing. He has a black coat with short, silky hair.

Fritter likes to...

  • Play with his big brother, Biscuit
  • Lay on the endge of the bed with one leg hanging off
  • Stare at the fish tank
  • Stetch his legs

A day in the life of our cats

Here is a small look into our cats daily schedule

  1. Meow at mommy and daddy's door until they open up
  2. Eat breakfast
  3. Run around the house as loudly as possible
  4. Nap with mommy
  5. Eat their leftovers (if there are any)
  6. Nap until daddy gets home from work
  7. Play until it's dinner time
  8. Play some more
  9. Cuddle with mom and dad until it's time to go to bed

I hope you enjoyed getting to know a little bit about my cats. I enjoyed telling you about them
